Yes.

What are the various methods to take the appointment?

Patient or his/her attendant can get the online appointment (over internet) by visiting our website or can come to appointment-cum-registration counter in the hospital.

What are the advantages of taking online appointment using our website?

Patients can avoid long queues at the hospital for seeking appointment /registration.
